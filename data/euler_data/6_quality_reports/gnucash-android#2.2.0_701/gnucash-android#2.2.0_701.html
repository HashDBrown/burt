1 Bug Report Information

GnuCash v. 2.2.0 #701
Split memos disappear
#### Steps to reproduce the behaviour
1. Click on transaction
1. Click on edit FAB button
1. Click on split icon
1. Add memo to 1 or more splits
1. Click "save"
1. Click "save" again
1. Click on edit FAB button
1. Click on split icon

#### Expected behaviour
Splits have their memos.

#### Actual behaviour
Split do not have their memos anymore.

#### Software specifications
* GnuCash Android version: 2.2.0
* System Android version: 6.0
* Device type: Any
