Bug summary:

Attempt to subscribe to WSJ Tech News Briefing via iTunes search fails, yields red flag instead of green flag.

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

Cannot add WSJ Tech News Briefing to podcast subscriptions (via "Subscriptions" item in the dropdown queue). The app allows you to search iTunes and click "Subscribe" for the podcast, but after saying "downloading" the "Subscribe" button comes back, and when you navigate backwards to the "Subscriptions" page, the WSJ Tech News Briefing podcast shows up with a red flag in the upper right hand corner.

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

When you search for the WSJ Tech News Briefing podcast in the "Subscriptions" section of the dropdown queue, you should be able to click "Subscribe" and the "Subscribe" button should change to say "Open Podcast." Then, when you navigate back to the "Subscriptions" page, the WSJ Tech News Briefing podcast should show up with a green flag in the upper right hand corner.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Click "Queue" bars at top left of screen

2. Click "Subscriptions"

3. Click "Add Podcast"

4. Click "Search iTunes"

5. Type "tech" in search bar, hit "Enter," click top result, WSJ Tech News Briefing

6. Click "Subscribe"

7. Navigate back four screens to "Subscriptions" page to see red flag
