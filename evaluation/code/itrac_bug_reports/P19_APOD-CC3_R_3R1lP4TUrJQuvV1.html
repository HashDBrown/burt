Bug summary:

Incorrect Behavior in Application

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

When a new podcast added to subscriptions it shows a red label in the corner.

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

When a new podcast added to subscriptions it should show a blue label in the corner.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Open the side menu by clicking the hamburger button on the left corner.

2. Click Subscriptions

3. Click Add Podcast

4. Click Search iTunes

5. Choose a podcast and click subscribe

6. Go back to Subscriptions and see the label of the podcast
