Bug summary:

Subscribed channel's ribbon is red instead of blue.

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

After subscribing to a channel, going back to Subscriptions page, the added channel appears on screen but with a red ribbon on its top right.

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

The color of the ribbon should be blue.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Tap on Hamburger menu and tap on Subscriptions.

2. Tap on "Add Podcast".

3. Tap "Search itunes.".

4. Tap on the magnifying glass icon on top right screen to search.

5. Enter "tech" with keyboard and then tap magnifying icon on keyboard.

6. Tap on one of the search results.

7. Tap on "Subscribe".

8. Tap on Back button of app 4 times to go back to Subscriptions page.
