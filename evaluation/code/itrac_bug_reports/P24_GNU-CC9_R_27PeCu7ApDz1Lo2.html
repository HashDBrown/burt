Bug summary:

Balance sheet entries get duplicated

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

After visiting the settings screen and then going back to reports page, the balance sheet entries get duplicated.

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

Balance sheet entries should remain as it is.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Tap Next on Welcome screen.

2. Select default currency and tap Next.

3. Select "Create default accounts" and tap Next.

4. Select "Automatically send crash reports" and tap Next.

5. Tap Done.

6. Tap Allow.

7. Tap on Hamburger menu button and tap Reports.

8. Tap on Sheet.

9. Tap on Hamburger menu button and tap Settings.

10. Tap on Back button.
