Bug summary:

The color of the top right chevron is red when it should be new on podcasts' album covers

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

When I add a new subscription to my list of subscriptions, the little chevron in the top right corner of the podcast's album cover is red when it should be blue

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

When I add a new subscription to my list of subscriptions, the little chevron in the top right corner of the podcast's album cover should be blue

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. From the main screen, select the hamburger option icon on the top left corner of the app to open the drawer

2. Select "Subscriptions" from the displayed options

3. Click "Add Podcast" on the Subscriptions page

4. Click the "Search Itunes" button

5. Select a podcast from the list that appears

6. Hit the "Subscribe" button on the podcast's page

7. Hit the back up in the top left corner until you arrive back at the "Subscriptions" page
