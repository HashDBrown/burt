Bug summary:

The app crashes when you try to duplicate a plant

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

Beginning with a cold start, after adding a new plant (named flower and of strain flower), the app crashes when you attempt to duplicate the plant.

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

I expected the plant to be successfully duplicated. The app should not have crashed.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Start the app

2. Allow Tracker to access photos, media, etc

3. Tap the red button with the plus icon

4. Name the plant 'Flower'

5. Specify that the plant is of strain 'Flower'

6. Hit the green check at the upper left corner

7. Tap on the newly created flower

8. Tap the three dots in the upper right corner

9. Tap Duplicate

10. The app stops working

Additional information:

This bug was encountered on a cold start of the application. There were no other plants.
