Bug summary:

When attempting to define a new additive when feeding a plant, the app crashes.

Observed Behavior (a description of what happened with the app):

When I attempted to create a new additive for plant feeding, the app crashed when I pressed "OK."

Expected Behavior (a description of what you expected to happen):

When creative a new additive and clicking "OK," the app should return to the Feed screen and allow the user to continue entering details, with the desired additive added to the list of additives.

Steps to Reproduce (the steps to reproduce the problem):

1. Create a plant with a name and a strain.

2. Click on the plant to access its details, then click on the Feed menu.

3. Click "New Additive," then click OK. The app crashes.
